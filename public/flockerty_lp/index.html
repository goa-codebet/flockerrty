The goal with Flockerty is to help people avoid crowded places and at the same time allow business owners to keep their business open.

What is Flockerty?

The idea is to let users register where they are going to be at a certain time, this could be a restaurant, supermarket, park etc. With this information from a large group of people we can generate a realtime heat-map of how crowded the places are going to be at a certain time, allowing other users to choose a time slot where the location is less crowded.
